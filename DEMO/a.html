About Us

Schnell Technocraft is an IT C & S (Consulting & Services) company that is focused on delivering consulting and technology services. Schnell is led and supported by team of seasoned IT professionals with more than 35+ years of combined multi-faceted IT experience.
Our C3 Mantra with "Commitment, Collaboration and Compentency" as the Core values, the company believes in exceeding customer expectations.

Why Schnell Technocraft?

Technology changes are major disruptors and unlike a slow paced human evolution, technology evolution is rapid, and specifically this era will see landmark changes in how organizations will evolve and technology will revolutionize way they conduct business.

For the last decade, the speed of technology advancements have forced the organizations to drifted away from the model of "staying informed" to "staying involved". This triggers a never ending process of not only keeping tab of the changes but also adopting to these changes and aligning this to the larger vision.

This stems the need of a "trusted" partner who could provide continuous insights and unbiased advice in and around these changes and allow organizations to stride, allowing them to focus on their core competencies.

Schnell Technocraft is led by experienced and passionate leaders who carry over three decades of combined IT consulting, designing and service delivery experience and have worked across several business verticals with a Mission - "To become a trusted IT partner".

Technology fuels us and so does a happy customer!!!
